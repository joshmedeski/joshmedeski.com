Welcome to your journey into **build your own** terminal-based development environment.

In this course, you'll learn how to set up your own environment and, more importantly, taught the skills required to **customize your workflow** to your liking. Here is a summary of the tools you'll be learning.

- alacritty: the fastest terminal emulator
- fish: a smart and user-friendly command line shell
- tmux: a terminal multiplexer
- neovim: a hyperextensible Vim-based text editor

By the end of the course, you will have your have all of these things set up.
