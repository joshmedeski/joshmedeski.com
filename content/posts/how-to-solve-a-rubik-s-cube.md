One of my earliest videos: a straightforward, beginner-friendly guide to solving a standard 3x3 Rubik's Cube.

I walk through the layer-by-layer method one step at a time — solving the first layer, then the middle, and finally the last layer — so you can go from scrambled to solved without memorizing pages of notation.
