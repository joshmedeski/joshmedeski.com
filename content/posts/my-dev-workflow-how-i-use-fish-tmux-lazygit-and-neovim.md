This is a quick, raw walkthrough of my development workflow — all of it living in the terminal. I combine a few tools that each do one job well:

- [**fish**](https://fishshell.com) as my interactive shell
- [**tmux**](https://github.com/tmux/tmux) for managing terminal sessions and windows
- [**lazygit**](https://github.com/jesseduffield/lazygit) for fast, visual version control
- [**Neovim**](https://neovim.io) as my editor

Together they let me move quickly without leaving the keyboard. Watch the video for a tour of how it all fits together.
